The codes to generate the look-up table / interpolation data lay
in this folder.

Generate_Interp_bladePitch_Control -
to generate the gain scheduled blade pitch control 
interpolation data.

Generate_Interp_PowerSpeedCurve_constp -
to generate the rotor speed reference for down-regulation
based on the torque law with constant power above rated conditions

Generate_Interp_PowerSpeedCurve_constt -
to generate the rotor speed reference for down-regulation
based on the torque law with constant torque above rated conditions
